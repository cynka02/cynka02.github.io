Quick tutorial
In this game you need to collect tons of letters.
Your main goal is to unlock and collect each letter of the alphabet!

To start with, collect 3 letters e.
To do this you have to enter it from your keyboard, press enter and wait!
Quick tutorial
Ok, that's enough.
Let's check the shop, which has been unlocked for you as reward!
(Click the menu or press right arrow on keyboard)
Quick tutorial
Now, unlock next letter - a.
While unlocking letters or upgrading their storage, you need to type appropriate text and collect necessary letters.
For example, hover over the key a. Do you see the sequence ee? That means you need to collect x2 of letter e and type it - commonly more than one time.
(After you type sth, useful info is displayed below the text field)

Unlock next letters and enjoy!
